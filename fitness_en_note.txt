Introduction

Fitness rooms are provided by the Leisure and Cultural Services Department (LCSD) in the urban area and the New Territories. Individuals aged 15 or above with one of the following qualifications may register as a User of LCSD Fitness Rooms for hire of fitness equipment:-

Participants who have completed the "Briefing on Proper Ways to Use Fitness Equipment" and passed the assessment organised by LCSD;
Participants who have completed the "Fitness (Multi-gym) Training Courses" organised by LCSD from May 2006 onwards with 80% or higher attendance rate ;
Holders of LCSD Fitness Gold Card; or
Holders of equivalent qualifications recognized by the Physical Fitness Association of Hong Kong, China.
